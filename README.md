# Breaking Bad

![Breaking Bad Poster](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/6/61/Breaking_Bad_title_card.png)

## Overview
**Breaking Bad** is an American crime drama television series created by *Vince Gilligan*.  
It aired from **2008 to 2013** and is widely regarded as one of the greatest TV shows of all time.

## Plot Summary
The story follows **Walter White**, a high school chemistry teacher who turns to cooking methamphetamine after being diagnosed with terminal lung cancer.  
With the help of his former student **Jesse Pinkman**, Walter descends deeper into the criminal underworld.
